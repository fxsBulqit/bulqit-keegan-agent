BULQIT POOL & OUTDOOR SERVICE - KNOWLEDGE BASE

=== WHAT BULQIT IS ===

Bulqit is a local neighborhood service coordinator for Encino and Sherman Oaks.
We organize groups of nearby homes to share outdoor services like lawn care, pool cleaning, pest control, and trash bin cleaning.
By coordinating neighbors together, everyone pays less and service providers waste less time driving around.

Launch: Full service begins in 2026
Current Status: Pre-launch enrollment and community building
